Everything you need to know about PremiumTV

IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) delivers television content over the internet instead of traditional cable or satellite. With PremiumTV, you simply connect to the internet, install our app or player on your device, and start streaming thousands of channels instantly. It works on any device including Smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, Fire Stick, and more. You get better quality, more channels, and pay significantly less than traditional cable.

PremiumTV works on virtually all devices including: Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony), Android TV, Fire TV/Stick, Apple TV, Roku, MAG boxes, Formuler devices, Android smartphones and tablets, iPhones and iPads, Windows and Mac computers, and web browsers. One subscription works on multiple devices, so your entire family can watch simultaneously (depending on your plan).

For smooth streaming, we recommend a minimum of 10 Mbps for HD quality and 25 Mbps for 4K Ultra HD content. However, our adaptive streaming technology adjusts quality based on your connection speed automatically, ensuring the best possible experience. Most home internet connections today are more than sufficient for excellent IPTV streaming.
