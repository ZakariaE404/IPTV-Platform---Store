Frequently Asked Questions

What is IPTV?

IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) allows you to stream live television channels and video-on-demand content over your internet connection instead of traditional satellite or cable.

Does it work on my device?

Yes! Our service works on Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony), Android boxes, Firestick, Apple TV, iOS, Android phones, PC, Mac, and Mag boxes.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 24-hour trial so you can test our channel quality and server stability before committing to a full subscription.

Is buffering guaranteed free?

We use high-bandwidth dedicated servers with anti-freeze technology. As long as you have a stable internet connection (min 15-20 Mbps), you will have a buffering-free experience.
